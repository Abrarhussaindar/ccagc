WHY IS CAREER GUIDANCE IMPORTANT FOR STUDENTS?
  • Career is a significant part of our lives today and any decision in terms of career planning now has to be taken with the utmost attention. With numerous courses, certifications, colleges, job opportunities available, choosing the right career path could become intimidating for students.
  • An adequate choice of career will not only offer an effective job opportunity but allows a student to embrace a satisfactory and respectful lifestyle.
  • Career counselling and guidance cell helps in empowering the students towards making the right career choices early on in their lives.
  • Career counselling and guidance process involves students exploring various career options, understanding more about the opportunities, analysing the career prospects and earning potential.
BENEFITS OF CAREER COUNSELLING AND GUIDANCE
  • You clarify your career goals.
  • It helps you understand your career options.
  • Determining strengths, interests and values.
  • Setting direction and goals.
  • Discards confusion.
  • Support and motivation.
  • You broaden your perspective.
GUIDELINES
Step1: The CCGC will arrange for talks/seminar by the experts periodically to help students choose their career options.
Step2: Interested students should register for the talk/seminar through the registration link shared by CCGC
Step3: All the registered students should proactively participate in the event conducted
Step4: A feedback will be collected from the students who participated in the event.
Step5: If any student requires personal counselling, he/she must fill registration form given in the Annexure-I below.
Step6: We use this form to learn more about you and how we can best serve you during your appointment.
Step7: After the counselling session, the student must fill the feedback form regarding the personal counselling session
