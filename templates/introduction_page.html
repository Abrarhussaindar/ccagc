INTRODUCTION

Career counseling is a process that focuses on helping one understand one's own self, so that one can take an informed decision about career and education. Career guidance is the guidance given to individuals to help them acquire the knowledge, information, skills, and experience necessary to identify career options, and narrow them down to make one career decision. Career counseling helps them understand the career options that they have, and how to pursue them.

Career counseling also helps them understand their own strengths and weaknesses with regard to their present course or profession, and lets them know what career they would be suited for. Career guidance helps individuals make a shift from the general understanding of life and work to a more specific understanding of the realistic and practical career options that are available to them.

OBJECTIVES
  • To promote discipline, healthy outlook and positive attitudes among students on placements.
  • Motivate students to take up jobs as per the current market trends and availability.
  • To conduct a survey among students on their career options.
  • To create awareness about the importance of higher studies in India and Abroad.
  • To conduct career development seminars and workshops.
  • To gather information on internship opportunities in different industrial segments
  • Help students to acquire/meet the expectations from industry by bringing industry people to university
  • Address students so that students will know what is expected of them when they take up a job.
  • To work closely with the placement team and identify the gaps that the university needs to fill/ bridge
  • And communicate the same to schools/ departments.
